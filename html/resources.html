Forms of Treatment

There are many forms of therapy for those who suffer from mental illnesses - both psychotherapeutic ("talk therapy") and psychopharmacological ("medication"). Psychotherapy is highly effective for a majority of those suffering from illnesses such as depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and many other mood and thought disorders. Medications may or may not be helpful but can help the recovery process. A psychiatrist is your best resource for adding medication and finding the one that is right for you. An important thing to remember is that rapport is important when finding a provider for therapeutic and psychiatric services. You may need to try different therapists, psychiatrists, medications, and talk-therapy modalities before finding the best path forward. The following section will highlight the forms of talk therapy that are available and activities that can be done at home to get well. An important message: stick with your quest to get well. It may take time and require that you try different paths.

Strategies for Staying Well
  • Be your own advocate. You have a voice and power to address your symptoms and illness.
  • Seek care from professionals. Therapists, psychologists, and psychiatrists are trained to help you.
  • Manage your stress by deep breathing, practicing yoga, and finding activities you enjoy.
  • Slow down and know your limits. If you need to take a break, take a break. Be willing to say no.
  • Be aware of your triggers.
  • Get adequate sleep. This may mean setting firm sleep schedule.
  • Follow a healthy diet. Not only can this make you feel better physically, a proper diet nourishes your brain.
  • Avoid alcohol and other illicit drugs.
  • Get regular exercise. This can be as simple as taking a walk.
  • Avoid isolating yourself. Spend time with friends or attend a group therapy session
  • Keep a journal. Writing down our emotions can be an effective release.
  • Get a coloring book or download and print coloring pages for free. Coloring can access your creativity and help you feel good about yourself.
